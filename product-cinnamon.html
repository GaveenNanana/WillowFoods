Ceylon Cinnamon

Ceylon Cinnamon, known as 'true cinnamon,' is sourced from the inner bark of Cinnamomum verum trees grown in Sri Lanka. Unlike common cassia cinnamon, Ceylon cinnamon has a sweet, delicate flavor with subtle citrus notes and lower coumarin content, making it safer for regular consumption.
