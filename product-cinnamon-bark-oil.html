Cinnamon Bark Oil

Steam-distilled from the bark of Cinnamomum verum trees grown in Sri Lanka, our Cinnamon Bark Oil is prized for its exceptionally high cinnamaldehyde content (65–75%). Rich, warm, and intensely aromatic — it is used across food flavoring, fragrance, cosmetics, and pharmaceutical industries.
